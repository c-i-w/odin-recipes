Stone Baked Pizza

Cooked to perfection, stone baked pizza is the perfect way to enjoy a slice

Ingredients

  • Home made or shop bought pizza
  • Pizza Oven or Pizza Stone for the ultimate experience

Recipe Instructions

  1. Prepare the choosen pizza type
  2. Insert into the pizza Oven
  3. Optional if using a pizza stone, introduce to the heat slowly as to not shock the stone
